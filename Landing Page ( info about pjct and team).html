The Secret Adventure

You cannot deny that the sea is full of thieves, full of pirates trying to uncover the mysteries of this land in order to get the ultimate reward, Captain beards long lost treasure. The myth says that only those whose hearts are made of gold will be worthy of the treasure and the title of the the Legend. Do you think you got what it takes to be a Legend. Jump in now

Our game is a secret treasure hunt that only exists in myths. From the moment you start the game you will be given riddles and questions to figure out and answer and by answering correctly you will be able to unlock the next level.
